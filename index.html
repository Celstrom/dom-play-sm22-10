This page is another example of how JavaScript can be useful on webpages. By adding an event listener on click of certain aspects of the page (in this case when you click on one of the lines of script) certain lines will be highlighted, making it easier to know which lines are yours to read. This is done by adding a function with if and else statements, if the highlight is yellow, then turn it to white and if the highlight it white, turn it to yellow. By adding an alert, when you click on a line, the character who is speaking that line will pop up as an alert, in this case, when you click on a line it will either trigger a popup saying "Gollum", or "Smeagol".
